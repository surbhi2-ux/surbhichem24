End-Use Applications:

Key applications of methyl levulinate include:

Fragrances and Flavors: Used as a flavor and fragrance ingredient in perfumes, cosmetics, and food products due to its pleasant odor and low toxicity.
Solvents: Employed as a green solvent in various industrial applications, including coatings, paints, and cleaning products.
Bio-Based Chemicals: Acts as an intermediate in the synthesis of bio-based chemicals and materials, contributing to the production of biodegradable plastics, resins, and additives.
Pharmaceuticals: Utilized in pharmaceutical formulations and as a precursor for active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs).
